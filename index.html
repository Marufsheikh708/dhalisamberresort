Our Story

Dhalis Amber Nivaas

The art of hospitality

A Legacy of Luxury

Hospitality has always been an art form at Dhali’s Amber Nivaas Limited. From the beginning of our journey in June 2017, Dhali’s Amber Nivaas Limited. has been offering its customers at an affordable price without compromising the 5-star standard service that has continuously created Dhali’s Amber Nivaas Limited, a very successful eco-friendly resort in Munshiganj, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

Dhali’s Amber Nivaas Ltd. was constructed in 2015 and opened its doors to welcome the very first guests in June 2017 by setting up with only 8 cottages and 60 luxurious tower rooms adjacent to the largest swimming pool in Bangladesh. It continues expanding by adding another lakeside cottage named Water Lily, with the capacity of 15 lavish suites and premium rooms, will be ready to serve from the beginning of 2021. From the opening to the present, Dhali’s Amber Nivaas Ltd. has earned a great reputation in its community and became a rising star in the hospitality business. The concept and the creation have already given a great vibe to the valued guests, partners and within the team. Our resort has already been voted number one based on all organic guest experiences and reviews from all the platforms.
